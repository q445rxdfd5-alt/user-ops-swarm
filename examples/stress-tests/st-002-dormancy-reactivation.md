# ST-002: Dormant Member Reactivation — Q3 Campaign

> **Priority**: MEDIUM — Quarterly target review, planning for July campaign
> **Submitted by**: Membership & CRM Lead
> **Date**: 2026-05-08

---

## Situation

We have **86,000 dormant members** (60+ days inactive). Previous campaigns sent the same message to everyone and got 3.1% conversion. The CFO has set a target: reactivate 12,000 dormant members in Q3 (July–September) at ≤¥3.50 CPA, contributing at least ¥180K in incremental GMV.

We know from Q4 2024 failure that segmentation matters. Our dormant members fall into three cohorts based on last-order recency:

| Cohort | Dormancy Duration | Count | Prior LTV | Reactivation Difficulty |
|--------|------------------|-------|-----------|----------------------|
| A — Lapsed | 60–90 days | 31,000 | ¥94 avg | Easiest — memory still fresh |
| B — Stale | 91–180 days | 28,000 | ¥67 avg | Medium — needs stronger offer |
| C — Deadwood | 180+ days | 27,000 | ¥38 avg | Hardest — may be churned |

The challenge: Cohort C members likely left for a reason. Spending ¥3.50 CPA to win back a ¥38 avg LTV customer is a money-loser. But blindly excluding them means ignoring 31% of the dormant file.

---

## Goals (Ranked)

1. Achieve 12,000 total reactivations (across all cohorts)
2. Maintain blended CPA ≤ ¥3.50
3. Ensure blended LTV of reactivated members ≥ ¥89 over 90-day window
4. Build segmented reactivation playbook reusable for future quarters
5. Generate at least ¥180K in incremental GMV from reactivated members (¥15 avg order × 12K × 1.0 reorder rate)

---

## Constraints

- **CPA ceiling**: ¥3.50 blended across all cohorts
- **Discount floor**: Cannot go below 35% combined discount on reactivation offers
- **One-to-one channel**: Primary channel is WeChat member notifications; secondary is SMS (¥0.08/sms cost)
- **Offer types allowed**: Points bonus, discount券, free add-on, early access to new menu items
- **Do not contact Cohort C**: Option under debate — excludes 31% of dormant file but protects CPA
- **No external DSP**: Member data cannot leave WeChat ecosystem

---

## Campaign Options Under Debate

**Option A — "Segmented Trio"**: Run three separate campaigns, one per cohort, with escalating offers:
- Cohort A: 10% off next order (brand-funded: 5%)
- Cohort B: 15% off + double points
- Cohort C: 25% off + free drink (brand-funded: 12%)
- Estimated blended CPA: ¥3.80 (slightly over budget)

**Option B — "Cohort A Focus"**: Drop Cohort C entirely, run aggressive Cohort A campaign only:
- Cohort A: 20% off (brand-funded: 10%)
- Cohort B: 15% off (brand-funded: 5%)
- Estimated CPA: ¥2.90 blended, but only ~60% of 12K target achievable from A+B
- **Gap**: Need 4,800 more reactivations — where do they come from?

**Option C — "Game Mechanics"**: No deep discounts. Instead, run a points-earning challenge (e.g., "Order twice in July, earn 500 bonus points = ¥5 value"):
- Lower immediate cost, drives behavioral activation
- Works best for Cohort A and B
- CPA estimated at ¥1.80 but conversion rate expected to drop to 4–5% (vs 8–12% for discount offers)

**Option D — "Aggressive C Scrap"**: Exclude Cohort C, aggressively push Cohort A and B, accept we won't hit 12K:
- Most conservative on CPA (¥2.40 blended)
- Achieves ~8,000 reactivations — misses target by 4,000
- CFO will ask why we're leaving 4,000 reactivation targets on the table

---

## Known Data Points

- Each percentage point of conversion across 86K members = 860 reactivation events
- At ¥15 avg order: every 7 reactivated members = ¥105 GMV; every 11 = ¥165 GMV → against CPA of ¥3.50, need ~21 reactivated members per ¥75 CPA spend to breakeven on CPA alone (before LTV)
- Previous 90-day reactivation campaigns at ¥3.20 CPA achieved 8% conversion on Cohort A with 10% off offer
- Our WeChat open rate for member notifications averages 18% across campaigns

---

## Critical Question

Which combination of cohort targeting, offer depth, and channel strategy achieves 12,000 reactivations at ≤¥3.50 CPA — or tells us honestly whether the target is achievable and what to do about the gap?

## Anti-Goals

- Do not send the same message to all 86K dormant members
- Do not run a blanket 25%+ discount — it destroys margin and trains members to wait for deep discounts
- Do not exclude Cohort C without explicit data justification
- Do not report CPA without also tracking 90-day LTV — CPA alone is a vanity metric
