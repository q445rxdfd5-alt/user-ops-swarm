### M20260514-obj2-smo | 2026-05-14 | operational_pattern

**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T19:38:14.211269
**Approved By**: HUMAN
**Scenario**: ST-001 Delivery Volume Crisis — Meituan Ranking Recovery
**Segment**: Platform Ranking Crisis / Operational Excellence
**Channel**: Meituan (Primary)
**Offer**: Fulfillment Excellence (No Discount)
**Outcome**: Decision: **APPROVE** (see runs/obj2-smoke-test/07_final_decision.md)
**Lesson**: The most effective recovery mechanism in platform ranking crises is operational excellence, NOT promotional discounting. Stores maintaining >93% fulfillment for 7 consecutive days recover ranking within 14 days (verified by platform account manager). The Chengdu Q4 2025 'quiet excellence' precedent worked because it prioritized fulfillment over promotions. Discounts fix volume but not root cause (kitchen overload), and can exacerbate the problem by triggering March 2024 failure patterns (capacit
**Reuse Condition**: Reuse for any future delivery platform ranking crisis (Meituan/Ele.me/Didi) where root cause is fulfillment-related (kitchen overload, staffing gaps, operational delays). Do NOT reuse when root cause is competitive displacement or seasonal demand drop requiring demandGeneration vs operational fixing
